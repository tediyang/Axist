AXIST

Axist is a transportation web application that guides users when moving from one location in
their city to another within their city. It provides the total distance covered, time taken, and
a map view to be aware of places. Axist also provides users with options for saving
several locations for easy navigation.

VISION

To revolutionize the way people commute, connecting individuals and communities seamlessly through
our innovative transportation app, enhancing convenience, efficiency, and sustainability.

MISSION

Our mission is to provide a cutting-edge transportation app that empowers users to navigate their daily lives
with ease. By leveraging technology and prioritizing user experience, we aim to offer a comprehensive platform
that integrates various transportation options, reduces congestion, promotes sustainability, and enhances
the quality of life for individuals and communities. Through our commitment to excellence, reliability, and continuous
innovation, we strive to become the go-to transportation solution for people worldwide.
