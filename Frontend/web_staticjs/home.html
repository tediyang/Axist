Axist is a transportation web application that helps users move from one location in their
city to another within their city. It provides the total distance covered, time
taken, a map showing the direction, and much more.

Easy Navigation:

Axist provides checkpoints within the total distance covered,
enabling efficiency and accuracy in making directions easier and
more understood. It gives the estimated time required to accomplish
the checkpoints.

Be Secured:

Axist provides a live map showing the directions to your
destination, giving you a vivid picture of pathways.

Get there on time:

Whether for personal or business purposes, getting there on time
is our top priority. Therefore, Axist provides you with alternative
and faster routes to specified locations.
